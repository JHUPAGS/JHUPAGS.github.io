We are the Johns Hopkins Physics and Astronomy Graduate Students (PAGS) association, and this is our new homepage! Our roles here include graduate student advocacy, outreach, and general support for our fellow students. Basically, we want to keep what’s working working, and to fix what can be fixed! If you have any ideas or questions about physics and astronomy graduate student life at JHU, feel free to contact any of us.

If you have a question and don’t know who to contact, see our organizational chart for information on who is in charge of what.
